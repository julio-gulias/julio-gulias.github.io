About the Channel

Practical experience, honest reflection

The channel will explore project and delivery leadership through practical experience, including the decisions, uncertainties, setbacks, and lessons that rarely fit neatly into a résumé or project plan.

It will also examine career transitions, periods between jobs, professional identity, immigration, technology, and the changing expectations placed on leaders and delivery professionals.

What to expect

Project & Delivery Leadership

Planning, governance, stakeholder alignment, delivery predictability, risk, and the practical realities of leading complex work.

Career Transitions

Lessons from layoffs, promotions, periods between jobs, rebuilding confidence, and navigating professional change.

Technology & the Future of Work

Reflections on artificial intelligence, digital transformation, changing roles, and how professionals can preserve critical thinking and autonomy.

Leadership & Professional Growth

Communication, self-awareness, decision-making, team motivation, and developing leadership through lived experience.
