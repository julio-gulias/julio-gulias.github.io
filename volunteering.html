PMI Espírito Santo · PMI Canadian West Coast Chapter

Mentoring has been one of the most meaningful parts of my volunteer journey. Through PMI chapters in Brazil and Canada, I've had the opportunity to support project managers and professionals who were looking for direction, growth, and practical guidance.
