Interactive Tool

Moving Motivators.

A Management 3.0 exercise built by Jurgen Appelo. Rank the ten intrinsic motivators from most to least important to you, then reflect on how well your current work supports each one — a simple way to turn a silent one-on-one into a real conversation.

Read the article View on LinkedIn ↗

How to use it

  1. Reveal: click a card to reveal which motivator it represents.
  2. Rank: drag and drop the cards to order them from the most important motivator to you (left) to the least important (right).
  3. Reflect: for each card, click + if your current work situation supports that motivator, or − if it works against it. Each click nudges the card up or down.
  4. Discuss: cards that end up high are strengths to build on; cards that sink low are worth talking about.

This tool is designed for web browsers on computers and is not optimized for mobile devices.
