About Me

The person behind the résumé

I'm a Brazilian-Canadian project and engagement manager with over 15 years of experience leading technology initiatives across four continents. My career started in software development at global companies in Brazil — HP and Dell — and then took me to Canada, where I've been building on that foundation in network infrastructure, enterprise software, and professional services.

What I've learned is that technology projects succeed or fail on the quality of the conversations around them. Getting the technical decisions right matters. Getting the team aligned, the client confident, and the risks visible matters just as much — often more.

I'm at my best when I'm navigating complexity: multiple stakeholders, evolving scope, distributed teams, and high expectations. I bring structure to ambiguous environments without turning structure into bureaucracy, and I've built a track record of turning struggling projects into high-performing ones.

Right now, I'm looking for my next role — ideally one where I can lead at the intersection of delivery, client success, and operational excellence.
