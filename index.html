About Me

The person behind the résumé

I'm a Brazilian-Canadian project, program, and delivery leader with over 15 years of experience leading technology initiatives across software development, infrastructure, enterprise software, and professional services. My career started in software development at global companies in Brazil — HP and Dell — and later brought me to Canada, where I've built on that foundation in network infrastructure, cybersecurity, digital platforms, and professional services.

What I've learned is that technology projects often succeed or fail on the quality of the conversations around them. Getting the technical decisions right matters. Getting the team aligned, the client confident, and the risks visible matters just as much — often more.

I'm at my best when I'm navigating complexity: multiple stakeholders, evolving scope, distributed teams, and high expectations. I bring structure to ambiguous environments without turning structure into bureaucracy, and I've built a track record of helping struggling projects become healthier, clearer, and more predictable.

Right now, I'm looking for my next role — ideally one where I can lead at the intersection of delivery, client success, and operational excellence.
